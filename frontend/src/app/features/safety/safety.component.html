Your Safety is Our Priority

How we keep Sasta Room safe for students and property owners.

Verified Profiles

Every owner and tenant profile is verified via government ID and contact checks to ensure a trustworthy community.

Manual Verification

Our ground team visits properties to verify their existence, amenities, and condition before they are listed.

Safe Visits

We provide guidelines for safe property visits and recommend meeting in daylight and notifying friends.

Reporting Fraud

If you encounter a suspicious listing or a broker posing as an owner, please report it immediately. Sasta Room is a zero-brokerage platform.

  • Never pay "visiting fees" to see a property.
  • Never share sensitive OTPs with strangers.
  • Always use our official platform for communication.
