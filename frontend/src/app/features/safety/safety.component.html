Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our highest priority. Follow these guidelines to have a safe and secure experience on Sasta Room.

Core Safety Principles

Verify Before You Visit

Always verify the property details, owner identity, and listing authenticity before scheduling a visit. Use our in-app verification badge as a guide.

Pay Only Through Platform

Never pay outside the Sasta Room platform. We do not support cash transactions. All payments are secured and documented for your protection.

Visit with Someone

When visiting a property for the first time, bring a friend or family member. Inform someone you trust about your visit location and expected return time.

Read the Agreement

Always read the rental agreement carefully before signing. Ensure all amenities, pricing, and cancellation policies are documented.

Report Suspicious Activity

If something seems off, trust your instincts. Report suspicious listings, requests for unusual payments, or uncomfortable interactions immediately.

Protect Your Data

Never share your password or OTP with anyone, including people claiming to be Sasta Room support. We will never ask for your password.

For Tenants

Do your research

Read reviews, check the Google Maps location, and look up the owner on our platform before committing.

Document everything

Take photos when you move in. Document any existing damage to avoid disputes when you move out.

Keep receipts

Save all payment receipts and written communications with the owner for future reference.

For Owners

Verify tenant identity

Always collect a copy of government ID before handing over keys. Sasta Room assists with this verification.

Use a proper agreement

Use our standard rental agreement template or consult a lawyer. A written agreement protects both parties.

Install safety equipment

Smoke detectors, emergency exits, and CCTV in common areas improve tenant safety and increase trust.

Emergency Contacts

If you feel unsafe or need immediate assistance, contact local emergency services.
