Owner Guidelines

Everything you need to know to be a great host on Sasta Room

Core Hosting Principles

Accurate Photos

Post only authentic, recent photos. Misleading photos lead to tenant disappointment and negative reviews. Use natural lighting and show all rooms.

Transparent Pricing

All charges (electricity, water, maintenance) must be mentioned upfront. No surprise costs after a tenant moves in.

Responsive Communication

Respond to inquiries within 24 hours. Owners with quick response rates get better ranking in search results.

Clean & Well-Maintained

Ensure the property is clean and all amenities are functional before a tenant moves in. Schedule regular maintenance.

Proper Agreements

Always use a written rental agreement. Sasta Room provides free agreement templates. Register agreements as required by law.

Tenant Privacy

Respecting tenant privacy is non-negotiable. Do not enter the property without prior notice except for genuine emergencies.

Required Documents

  • Property ownership proof (sale deed / khata)
  • Owner's government-issued ID (Aadhaar / PAN)
  • Clear, recent photos of all rooms
  • Bank account for rent transfers
  • GST registration (if applicable)
  • NOC from Society (if in a gated community)

Dos & Don'ts

Do's

Be honest about the property condition
Respond to queries promptly
Keep the property clean and maintained
Give proper receipts for deposits
Return deposits on time as per agreement
Report violations to Sasta Room support

Don'ts

Use fake or outdated photos
Discriminate tenants based on religion or gender
Ask for payments outside the platform
Enter property without tenant notice
Make unauthorized changes to listing prices
Withhold security deposits without reason
