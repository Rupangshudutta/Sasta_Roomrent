2. How We Use Your Data

  • To match you with suitable properties and connect you with owners
  • To process bookings and payments securely
  • To send booking confirmations and service updates
  • To personalize your search experience
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Property Owners: Your name and contact for confirmed bookings only
  • Payment Processors: Razorpay / Stripe for secure payment handling
  • Analytics Partners: Anonymized, aggregated data only
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential: Required for the Platform to function (session management)
  • Analytics: Help us understand how users interact with Sasta Room
  • Preferences: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may impact Platform functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and employee training

6. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sastaroom.in

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian financial regulations. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries:

  • Email: privacy@sastaroom.in
  • Grievance Officer: As required by IT Act, 2000
